About Heaps

Heap Visualizer is an educational tool designed to help students and developers understand how Min-Heap and Max-Heap data structures work. With interactive diagrams, simple explanations, and live examples, users can visualize how heap operations like insertion, deletion, and heapify affect the structure in real time.
